User Guide

How to get your connections file

Exporting from LinkedIn

Takes about 2 minutes. LinkedIn emails you a ZIP file with your data.

  1. Click your profile picture → Settings & Privacy
  2. Left sidebar → Data Privacy
  3. Click "Get a copy of your data"
  4. Tick Connections
  5. Click "Request archive" — check your email
  6. Download ZIP → extract → find Connections.csv
  7. Drop that CSV into the upload zone above ↑

What you get

A complete career-intelligence analysis of your entire network.

  1. 16 role categories — Founders, Recruiters, SWE, FAANG, YC, and more
  2. Growth timeline — see when your network grew
  3. Top companies — most represented employers
  4. Drill-down — click a tile → see all, search, paginate
  5. Excel workbook — dashboard with charts + 16 sheets
