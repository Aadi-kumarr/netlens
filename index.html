User Guide

How to get your connections file

Exporting from LinkedIn

Takes about 2 minutes. LinkedIn emails you a ZIP with your data — can take up to 1 hour.

  1. Click your profile photo → Settings & Privacy
  2. Left sidebar → Data Privacy
  3. Click "Get a copy of your data"
  4. Tick Connections → click Request archive
  5. Check your email → download the ZIP
  6. Extract it → find Connections.csv
  7. Drop that file into the upload zone above ↑

What you get

A complete intelligence analysis of your entire LinkedIn network.

  1. 16 role categories — Founders, Recruiters, SWE, FAANG, YC, and more
  2. Growth timeline — see exactly when your network grew
  3. Top companies — most represented employers
  4. Drill-down drawer — click a tile, search, paginate
  5. Clickable Excel workbook — dashboard + 15 category sheets
